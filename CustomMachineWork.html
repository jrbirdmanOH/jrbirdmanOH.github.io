Precision Milled Screws

D.C. Morrison is your Acme Thread Specialists.

Long-wearing, replacement screws on our precison screw-thread milling equipment meet all standards for accurace and machine tool specification.

All screws are checked using their precision-wire method.

What sets us apart is our ability to provide a variety of complex thread shapes in a variety of lengths.

We can provide screws

  • In virtually any pitch combination
  • With up to four starts
  • Thread-only or complete with journal
  • Infinite lengths

All screws are manufactured from high-quality blank screw stock. If needed, D. C. Morrison can fabricate replacement nuts.

Fast Turnaround

Working from either a sample or a print, we are able to turn most orders around within a week ‐ even faster for emergency situations.
